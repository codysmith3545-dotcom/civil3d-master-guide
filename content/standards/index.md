> **TL;DR**
> 1. Standards split into **design standards** (AASHTO, state DOT manuals), **survey standards** (ALTA/NSPS, state board rules), and **CAD/drafting standards** (NCS, AIA).
> 2. Most are **copyrighted** — we cite and summarize, not republish. Government-authored manuals (state DOT, federal) are public-domain and may be mirrored verbatim under [docs-mirror](../docs-mirror/index.md).
> 3. Always check the **current edition**; standards revise on a 5–10 year cycle.
