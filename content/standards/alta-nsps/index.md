> **TL;DR**
> 1. The **2021 ALTA/NSPS Minimum Standard Detail Requirements for ALTA/NSPS Land Title Surveys** is the current effective version (Feb 23, 2021).
> 2. The standard has **mandatory sections** (1–6) and **optional Table A items**. The client/title company picks Table A items at retention.
> 3. The 2021 standard is **freely downloadable** from NSPS — link it on every ALTA proposal so the client knows what they're paying for.
