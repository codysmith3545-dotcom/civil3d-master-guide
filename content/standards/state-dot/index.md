> **TL;DR**
> 1. State DOTs adopt AASHTO with state-specific modifications. **For Indiana, that's the INDOT Indiana Design Manual (IDM)** and the **INDOT Standard Specifications**.
> 2. INDOT publishes the IDM publicly online (chapters as PDFs); it can be mirrored verbatim as a government-authored work (see [docs-mirror](../../docs-mirror/index.md)).
> 3. Locally adopted standards (county/municipal) sit on top of INDOT — always check the local jurisdiction's design standards manual too.
