> **TL;DR**
> 1. Key AASHTO publications for civil/transportation work: **A Policy on Geometric Design of Highways and Streets** ("Green Book"), **Roadside Design Guide**, **MUTCD** (jointly with FHWA), **AASHTO LRFD Bridge Design Specifications**, **MASH** (crash testing).
> 2. AASHTO publications are **copyrighted and sold by AASHTO**. Verbatim text is not republished here. Cite section and edition.
> 3. The **Green Book 8th Edition (2024)** is the current edition as of this writing — verify before relying on it.
