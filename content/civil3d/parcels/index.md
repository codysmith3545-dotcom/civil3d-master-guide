> **TL;DR**
> 1. Parcels are 2D enclosed areas on a **site**. The site is the topology container — parcels in the same site share boundaries.
> 2. Sizing tools (slide line, swing line) snap parcel splits to a target area, depth, or width.
> 3. The right-of-way is usually parcel #0 in a subdivision — defining it first lets the lots inside cleanly subtract.
