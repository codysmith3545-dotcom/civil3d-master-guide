> **TL;DR**
> 1. Two flavors: **gravity** (storm/sanitary) and **pressure** (water/force-main). Different toolsets, different parts lists.
> 2. **Parts list** = the pipe + structure types available. **Pipe rules** = how pipes auto-connect and slope. **Structure rules** = how structures auto-elevate (rim, sump).
> 3. Networks render in plan, profile, and section. To show in profile, **add network to profile view** after creating both.
