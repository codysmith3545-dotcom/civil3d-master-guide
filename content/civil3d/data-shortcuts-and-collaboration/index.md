> **TL;DR**
> 1. Data shortcuts are XML pointers to Civil 3D objects in other drawings - lightweight, no server, no check-out.
> 2. Vault and BIM Collaborate (formerly BIM 360 Design / ACC Docs) add version control, locking, and audit trails on top of (or replacing) shortcuts.
> 3. The right tool depends on team size, multi-discipline coordination needs, and whether changes must be audited.
