> **TL;DR**
> 1. Surface analyses paint a TIN with elevation, slope, aspect, or watershed colors and produce a legend table.
> 2. Volume surfaces are a separate surface type that subtracts a base from a comparison surface and yields cut/fill quantities.
> 3. Surface editing - paste, simplify, smooth, weed - controls TIN quality and file size. Use each operation deliberately, not in bulk.
