> **TL;DR**
> 1. A **surface profile** samples an existing surface along an alignment. A **layout profile** is your design ground.
> 2. **Profile views** are the windows that draw profiles — alignment + profile-view band + grid + labels.
> 3. **K-value** controls vertical-curve length: `L = K × A`. Sag = headlight or comfort criterion; crest = sight-distance criterion. See AASHTO Tables 3-34 and 3-36.
