> **TL;DR**
> 1. An **alignment** is a horizontal centerline made of **lines**, **curves**, and (optionally) **spirals**. It carries stationing.
> 2. Use **design criteria files** (XML referencing AASHTO defaults) to validate radius, superelevation, and design speed automatically.
> 3. **Offset alignments** are dynamic to the parent — use them for edge of pavement, ROW, etc., so they update when the centerline changes.
