> **TL;DR**
> 1. **Feature lines** are 3D lines with elevation; they're the backbone of grading.
> 2. **Grading objects** project from a feature line baseline at a slope/distance to a target (surface, elevation, distance) — a **grading group** organizes them and produces a surface.
> 3. For complex sites, prefer **corridors** with a daylight subassembly over freehand grading objects — corridors update predictably; grading objects can be brittle.
