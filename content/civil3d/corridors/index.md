> **TL;DR**
> 1. A **corridor** is the 3D model produced by sweeping an **assembly** (cross-section) along an **alignment** + **profile** at a chosen **frequency**.
> 2. **Subassemblies** are the parts of an assembly (lane, curb, sidewalk, daylight). The **Subassembly Composer** builds custom ones; the stock tool palette ships ~200 generic ones.
> 3. **Targets** let subassemblies follow other geometry — surfaces (for daylighting), alignments (for variable widths), profiles (for variable elevations).
