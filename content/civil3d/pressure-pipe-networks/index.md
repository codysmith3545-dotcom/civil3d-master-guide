> **TL;DR**
> 1. Pressure pipe networks model water mains, force mains, and other full-flow systems where slope is not the design driver - elevation and depth-of-cover are.
> 2. Parts come from manufacturer content catalogs; rules enforce minimum cover, joint deflection, and segment length.
> 3. Use a pressure network when the design needs fittings, bends, and exact manufacturer geometry; convert to gravity only if you specifically need slope-driven analysis.
