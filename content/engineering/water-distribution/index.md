> **TL;DR**
> 1. Distribution systems are sized for the **larger** of: max-day demand + fire flow, **or** peak-hour demand. Maintain residual pressure ≥ 20 psi during fire flow.
> 2. **Velocity** target ≤ 5 ft/s during normal flow (≤ 10 ft/s during fire flow). **Min cover** is climate-driven; Indiana typically 4'–5' cover.
> 3. Fire-flow targets per ISO/AWWA depend on construction type and occupancy; typical residential is 1000–1500 gpm @ 20 psi for 2 hr.
