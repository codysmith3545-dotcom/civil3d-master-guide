> **TL;DR**
> 1. The deliverables: an **Erosion & Sediment Control Plan (ESCP)** + a **Stormwater Pollution Prevention Plan (SWPPP)** maintained on-site by the contractor.
> 2. The **non-negotiables**: stabilized construction entrance, perimeter sediment controls (silt fence / sediment basins), inlet protection, slope stabilization, concrete washout, dewatering control.
> 3. In Indiana, projects ≥ **1 acre** disturbed area trigger **IDEM Rule 5** Notice of Intent (NOI). MS4 communities have additional local review.
