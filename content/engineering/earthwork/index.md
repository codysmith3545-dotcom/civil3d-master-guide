> **TL;DR**
> 1. Earthwork volume = the difference between two surfaces. Civil 3D's **TIN volume surface** is the cleanest method.
> 2. **Shrink/swell factors** (compaction adjustment) apply between in-place and embankment volumes — typical: 10–25% shrink for cohesive soils.
> 3. The **mass haul diagram** shows where to balance and where you need to import/export. Hauling is expensive; reshaping the design to balance is usually cheaper.
