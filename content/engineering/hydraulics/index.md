> **TL;DR**
> 1. Open-channel flow uses **Manning's equation**: `Q = (1.486/n) A R^(2/3) S^(1/2)`. `n` ranges from ~0.011 (smooth concrete) to ~0.10+ (heavy brush).
> 2. Pipe flow has two flavors: **gravity** (free-surface, Manning) and **pressure** (Hazen-Williams or Darcy-Weisbach).
> 3. **Culvert hydraulics** are controlled by either **inlet** (entrance loss, headwater) or **outlet** (full-pipe friction + tailwater) — design for the worse of the two.
