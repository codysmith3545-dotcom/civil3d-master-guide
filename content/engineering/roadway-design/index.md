> **TL;DR**
> 1. The U.S. baseline is **AASHTO "A Policy on Geometric Design of Highways and Streets"** (the "Green Book"). State DOT manuals (INDOT IDM in Indiana) modify and add to it.
> 2. Geometric design starts with **functional class** + **design speed** → driving everything else (radius, K, sight distance, lane width).
> 3. Always check the **three sight-distance** flavors: **stopping (SSD)**, **decision (DSD)**, **passing (PSD)** + intersection sight distance.
