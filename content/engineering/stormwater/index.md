> **TL;DR**
> 1. Stormwater design has **three** chained problems: **(1)** estimate inflow (hydrology), **(2)** convey it (hydraulics), **(3)** detain/treat it (BMPs).
> 2. For small drainage areas (< ~200 ac) the **Rational Method** is standard: `Q = CiA`. Bigger areas → **SCS/NRCS curve number** methods, often via **HEC-HMS** or **Storm and Sanitary Analysis**.
> 3. Indiana sites usually trigger **IDEM Rule 5** (construction stormwater) and may trigger **Rule 13** (MS4) — both administered by IDEM with municipal partners.
