> **TL;DR**
> 1. Gravity sewer is sized for **peak wet-weather flow** at **velocity ≥ 2 ft/s** when half-full and ≤ 10 ft/s when full. Min slope is the slope that achieves 2 ft/s at design flow (commonly 0.40% for 8" PVC, 0.22% for 10", 0.15% for 12").
> 2. Manholes at every change of direction, slope, size, material; **max spacing** is jurisdiction-specific (often 400 ft for ≤ 24" pipe).
> 3. **Indiana** generally follows the **Ten States Standards** (Recommended Standards for Wastewater Facilities) plus state and local amendments.
