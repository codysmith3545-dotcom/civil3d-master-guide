> **TL;DR**
> 1. The site-design ADA rules you apply most: **running slope ≤ 5%** along an accessible route (≤ 8.33% if it's a ramp with handrails), **cross slope ≤ 2%**, **landings 5'×5' min** at top/bottom and at direction changes.
> 2. **Curb ramps**: max 8.33% running, max 2% cross, **detectable warning** at the curb-line. Truncated dome panels are 24" deep min.
> 3. **PROWAG** (Public Right-of-Way Accessibility Guidelines, finalized 2023) governs sidewalks, curb ramps, transit stops in the public ROW.
