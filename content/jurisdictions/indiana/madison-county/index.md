> **TL;DR**
> 1. Madison County is northeast of Marion; **Anderson is the county seat**.
> 2. Municipalities include **Anderson, Pendleton, Elwood, Alexandria, Lapel, Edgewood, Chesterfield, Country Club Heights, Frankton, Ingalls, Markleville, Orestes, River Forest, Summitville**.
> 3. Drainage administered by the **Madison County Surveyor's Office** with the **Madison County Drainage Board**. Detailed content forthcoming; see municipal pages and the county website for current rules.
