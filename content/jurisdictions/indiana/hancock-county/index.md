> **TL;DR**
> 1. Hancock County is east of Marion; **Greenfield is the county seat**.
> 2. Municipalities: **Greenfield, Fortville, McCordsville, New Palestine, Cumberland (partially), Wilkinson, Shirley**.
> 3. Drainage is administered by the **Hancock County Surveyor's Office** with the **Hancock County Drainage Board**.

## Authorities

- **Hancock County government** — https://www.hancockcoingov.org/
- **Hancock County Surveyor** — drainage and regulated drains.
- **Hancock County Engineer (Highway Dept)**.
- **Hancock County Plan Commission**.
