> **TL;DR**
> 1. Morgan County is southwest of Marion; **Martinsville is the county seat**, **Mooresville** is the largest town.
> 2. Municipalities: **Martinsville, Mooresville, Brooklyn, Monrovia, Paragon, Morgantown** (the latter spans Brown County).
> 3. Drainage administered by the **Morgan County Surveyor** + Drainage Board. The **White River** corridor passes through the county.
