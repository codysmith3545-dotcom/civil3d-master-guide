> **TL;DR**
> 1. **INDOT** owns state highways and produces the **Indiana Design Manual (IDM)** + **Standard Specifications** + **Standard Drawings**.
> 2. **IDEM** administers stormwater (**Rule 5** for construction sites ≥ 1 ac, **Rule 13** for MS4 communities) and other environmental programs.
> 3. **PLA** licenses Professional Surveyors and Professional Engineers; the **State Board of Registration for Professional Surveyors** writes Indiana practice rules. **Indiana 811** is the call-before-you-dig service (statutorily required ≥ 2 working days notice).

## Key statutes & rules

- **IC 36-2-12** — County Surveyor; includes the **Surveyor's Report** statute (IC 36-2-12-14).
- **IC 8-1-26** — Underground Plant Protection (Indiana 811).
- **865 IAC 1-12** — Standards of Practice for Surveyors (Indiana Administrative Code).
- **327 IAC 15-5** — Rule 5 (construction stormwater).
- **327 IAC 15-13** — Rule 13 (MS4).

## Coordinate system

- **State Plane Indiana East — FIPS 1301**, transverse Mercator, NAD83 (and NAD83(2011)).
- **State Plane Indiana West — FIPS 1302**, transverse Mercator, NAD83 (and NAD83(2011)).
- **Geoid model** — Geoid18 (current) for orthometric heights (NAVD88).
- The 8 counties in this guide all fall in **Indiana East**.
