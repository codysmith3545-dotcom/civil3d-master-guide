> **TL;DR**
> 1. Hendricks County is west of Marion; **Danville is the county seat**, **Plainfield, Avon, Brownsburg** are the largest towns.
> 2. Municipalities: **Danville, Plainfield, Avon, Brownsburg, Pittsboro, Lizton, Stilesville, Coatesville, North Salem, Clayton, Amo**.
> 3. The county has its own **Stormwater Technical Standards Manual**.
