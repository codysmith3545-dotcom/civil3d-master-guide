> **TL;DR**
> 1. Boone County is northwest of Marion; **Lebanon is the county seat**. **Zionsville** and **Whitestown** are the largest growth areas.
> 2. Municipalities: **Lebanon, Zionsville, Whitestown, Thorntown, Advance, Jamestown, Ulen**.
> 3. Drainage administered by the **Boone County Surveyor** + Drainage Board.

## Authorities

- **Boone County government** — https://www.boonecounty.in.gov/
- **Boone County Surveyor**.
- **Boone County Engineer / Highway**.
- **Boone County Area Plan Commission** (joint with several municipalities).
