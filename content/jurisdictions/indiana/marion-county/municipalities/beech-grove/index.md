> **TL;DR**
> 1. Beech Grove is an excluded city inside Marion County, with its own zoning and planning authority.
> 2. Stormwater and engineering review goes through the **Beech Grove Engineering Department**; Marion County Surveyor still has drainage jurisdiction for regulated drains.
> 3. Sanitary sewer is typically Citizens Energy; water similarly. Verify per project.
