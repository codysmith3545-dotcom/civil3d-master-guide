> **TL;DR**
> 1. Indianapolis is consolidated with Marion County under Unigov; for sites in the Indianapolis (i.e., not in an excluded city/town) portion, **Indianapolis DPW + DBNS** review applies.
> 2. The **Indianapolis Stormwater Design and Specifications Manual** governs stormwater design within the consolidated city.
> 3. **Citizens Energy Group** provides water and sanitary sewer service in most of the city.

## Authorities

- **City of Indianapolis** — https://www.indy.gov/
- **DPW** — Engineering, Stormwater, ROW — https://www.indy.gov/agency/department-of-public-works
- **DBNS** — Permits, Plan Review, Zoning — https://www.indy.gov/agency/department-of-business-and-neighborhood-services
- **Marion County Surveyor** — regulated drains.
- **Citizens Energy Group** — water + sanitary.

## Codes & standards

- **Revised Code (Indianapolis-Marion County)** — https://library.municode.com/in/indianapolis_-_marion_county
- **Indianapolis Stormwater Design and Specifications Manual** — DPW.
- **Indianapolis ROW Standard Drawings**.

## Permits typically needed

- Drainage permit / stormwater plan approval (DPW).
- ROW / driveway permit (DPW).
- Sanitary connection (Citizens Energy Group).
- Improvement Location Permit / Building Permit (DBNS).
- IDEM Rule 5 NOI (≥ 1 ac disturbed).
