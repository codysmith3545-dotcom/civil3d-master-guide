> **TL;DR**
> 1. Shelby County is southeast of Marion; **Shelbyville is the county seat**.
> 2. Municipalities: **Shelbyville, Morristown, Fairland, St. Paul, Edinburgh** (Edinburgh and St. Paul span county lines).
> 3. Drainage is administered by the **Shelby County Surveyor** with the Drainage Board.
