> **TL;DR**
> 1. Johnson County is south of Marion; **Franklin is the county seat**, **Greenwood** is the largest city.
> 2. Municipalities: **Franklin, Greenwood, Whiteland, Bargersville, New Whiteland, Trafalgar, Princes Lakes, Edinburgh** (multi-county).
> 3. Drainage administered by the **Johnson County Surveyor** + Drainage Board.
