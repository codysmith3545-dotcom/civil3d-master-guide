## Status

The Indiana Board of Registration for Professional Surveyors administers continuing-education requirements for licensed surveyors (typically a fixed number of contact hours per renewal cycle, with subject-area requirements covering ethics, standards, and technical topics). Course providers must apply to the Board for approval; individual modules and instructors are approved separately.

This project has not yet submitted any module for CE approval. The pages below are study and reference material only.

## How CE approval typically works in Indiana

1. The provider applies to the Indiana Professional Licensing Agency (IPLA) for course approval, with a syllabus, learning objectives, and instructor qualifications.
2. The Board reviews; approved courses are assigned a course number and listed as approved.
3. Licensees claim credit on their renewal under the approved course number.
