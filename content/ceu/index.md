> 2. This site hosts an 8-module self-study CEU framework. Each module is sized for approximately **1 Professional Development Hour (PDH)**: 50 minutes of focused reading plus a 10-minute self-assessment.

## What CEU is, and how Indiana counts it

Continuing Education Units (CEU), in Indiana, are measured in **Professional Development Hours (PDH)**. The Board's expectation is that 1 PDH represents 50 minutes of structured learning, consistent with NCEES and the model rules followed by most state surveying boards.

Indiana's biennial renewal cycle, set out in IC 25-21.5 and the implementing administrative rules, requires **24 PDH per renewal**. Of those, a minimum of **2 PDH** must be in Indiana-specific content: typically 865 IAC 1-12, recent Indiana case law on boundary issues, or the practice rules administered by the Board.

Acceptable activity categories generally include:

- Live or recorded seminars hosted by a recognized provider (ISPLS, NSPS, ACEC-IN, INDOT, county surveyor offices).
- College or university coursework in surveying, geodesy, boundary law, or related fields.
- Authoring a published paper or technical article (with PDH credit assigned by topic).
- Active participation as an officer or committee member of a recognized professional organization.
- Approved self-study programs — which is the category this framework targets.

The Board does not pre-approve every activity; rather, it expects the licensee to keep records that justify the hours claimed. The licensee is responsible for retaining documentation for the period prescribed by the Board (commonly three years past the renewal cycle).

## How to use this framework as study material

Each module is a standalone reading: 800-1500 words of content, a learning-objective list, an embedded self-assessment, and cited sources. Suggested approach:

1. **Read the module top to bottom.** Take notes as you would in a live seminar. Aim for 45-50 minutes of focused reading.
2. **Complete the self-assessment.** Answer the 3-5 questions without looking back, then check your work against the answer key.
3. **Log completion in the tracker.** The [/ceu page](/ceu) records the module ID and completion date in your browser's local storage. No account is required.
4. **Print the completion report** at the end of your renewal cycle. The report lists each completed module, the date, and the PDH credit you would claim if the Board approves the curriculum.

## Status and disclaimer

This framework is **pending approval** by the Indiana State Board of Registration for Surveyors. Until the Board has:

- Reviewed each module for technical accuracy and currency.
- Assigned a provider identifier to this curriculum.
- Issued formal approval letters covering the renewal cycles in which it can be claimed.

...the hours documented here **do not count toward the 24 PDH renewal requirement**. Treat self-study completions as personal professional development, not credentialed CEU.

The authors are licensed surveyors and engineers, but nothing on this site constitutes legal advice or an official ruling from the Board. When in doubt, contact the Indiana Professional Licensing Agency.
