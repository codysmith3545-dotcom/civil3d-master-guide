> **TL;DR**
> 1. **Indiana licensure** is administered by the **Indiana Professional Licensing Agency (PLA)** through the State Board of Registration for Professional Surveyors and the Board of Registration for Professional Engineers.
> 2. The **NCEES** runs the national exams: **FS / PS** (Surveying), **FE / PE Civil** (Engineering). Indiana accepts NCEES results.
> 3. **Autodesk** offers product certifications: **Autodesk Certified Professional — Civil 3D**. Useful for resume; not licensing.

## Licensure (Indiana)

### Land Surveyor (PS)

- **Indiana State Board of Registration for Professional Surveyors** (under PLA): https://www.in.gov/pla/professions/surveyors-home/
- Path: degree → **FS exam** (NCEES) → 4 years SIT (Surveyor in Training) experience → **PS exam** (NCEES) + **State-Specific exam (SSE)** for Indiana.
- Indiana SSE covers Indiana boundary law, IC 36-2-12 (Surveyor's Reports), and state procedures.

### Professional Engineer (PE)

- **Indiana State Board of Registration for Professional Engineers** (under PLA): https://www.in.gov/pla/professions/engineering-home/
- Path: ABET-accredited degree → **FE exam** → 4 years experience → **PE Civil exam** (NCEES).

### Continuing Education

- Indiana surveyors and engineers must complete continuing-education hours per renewal cycle. Check current PLA rules.

## Autodesk certifications

- **Autodesk Certified Professional — Civil 3D** — proctored exam testing real workflows. Updated per release.
- **Autodesk Certified User — AutoCAD** — entry-level.

## Survey training providers

- **POB Online**, **xyHt** — webinars and continuing-education.
- **State society events** — ISPLS conferences include CE credits.

## Civil 3D training

- **Autodesk University** (AU) — free recorded sessions, annual conference.
- **LinkedIn Learning / Pluralsight** — Civil 3D courses, varied quality.
- **Cadapult Software**, **Civil 3D Reminders** — focused training programs.

## Indiana exam prep

- ISPLS publishes recommended-reading lists for the SSE.
- Self & Yoakam (and similar) publish state-specific study guides — check the latest edition.
