> **TL;DR**
> 1. ★ **Autodesk Civil 3D forums** — official, indexed, answered by both Autodesk staff and power users.
> 2. **r/Surveying** and **r/civilengineering** on Reddit — fast, candid, geographically diverse.
> 3. **Land Surveyors United** — large global community for surveyors specifically.

## Civil 3D

- ★ **Autodesk Civil 3D forum** — https://forums.autodesk.com/t5/civil-3d/ct-p/4003
- **Civil 3D subreddit** — https://www.reddit.com/r/civil3d (smaller, friendly).
- **AUGI Civil 3D forums** — https://www.augi.com (older but archive is searchable).

## Surveying

- ★ **Land Surveyors United** — https://landsurveyorsunited.com
- **r/Surveying** — https://www.reddit.com/r/Surveying
- **NSPS member groups** (state-level) — https://www.nsps.us.com
- **Indiana Society of Professional Land Surveyors (ISPLS)** — https://www.ispls.org (state society).
- **Stack Exchange — GIS** — https://gis.stackexchange.com (occasional surveying topics).

## Civil engineering

- **r/civilengineering** — https://www.reddit.com/r/civilengineering
- **EngineerBoards** — https://engineerboards.com (PE/FE exam-prep heavy).
- **ASCE Collaborate** — https://collaborate.asce.org

## Programming for AEC

- **The Swamp** (CAD coding forum) — https://www.theswamp.org
- **Stack Overflow** — tagged `autocad`, `civil-3d`, `dynamo`.
