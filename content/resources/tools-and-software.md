> **TL;DR**
> 1. Civil 3D rarely lives alone. **Trimble Business Center** (TBC) and **Carlson** are the companion office software for survey processing; **HEC-RAS / EPANET / EPA SWMM** for hydraulics; **AutoTURN** for vehicle paths; **ArcGIS / QGIS** for GIS context.
> 2. Free options exist for most paid tools — pick what your jurisdiction reviewers use to avoid file-format friction.
> 3. Field data collectors talk to almost everything via LandXML / CSV; if a piece of software doesn't, that's a sign.

## Survey office software

- **Trimble Business Center (TBC)** — paid. Strong for control adjustments + scan/UAS data.
- **Carlson SurvCE / SurvPC / Carlson Survey** — paid. Field + office package.
- **MicroSurvey / FieldGenius** — paid. Adjustments, point cloud, COGO.
- **STAR\*NET** — least-squares network adjustment. Paid.

## Hydraulics / hydrology

- ★ **HEC-RAS** — USACE, free. 1D + 2D river/floodplain modeling.
- ★ **EPA SWMM** — free. Stormwater conveyance + LID.
- **HEC-HMS** — USACE, free. Hydrology / runoff.
- **Storm and Sanitary Analysis (SSA)** — included with Civil 3D for years; functionality moved/limited in 2024+ — check current install.
- **PondPack / StormCAD / SewerGEMS** — Bentley OpenFlows. Paid.
- **EPANET** — EPA, free. Water-distribution hydraulics.
- **HydroCAD** — paid. SCS-method hydrology + pond routing. Common in NE US.

## Roadway / traffic

- **AutoTURN** — vehicle path simulation. Paid; widely required by reviewers.
- **VISSIM / Synchro** — traffic simulation/signal timing. Paid.

## GIS

- ★ **QGIS** — free, open source. Excellent for context maps and quick GIS work.
- **ArcGIS Pro / Online** — paid. Standard for utilities and municipal GIS.
- **Global Mapper** — paid, broad GIS + LiDAR.

## Drone / point cloud

- **Pix4D / DroneDeploy / Reality Capture** — photogrammetry. Paid.
- **Cyclone REGISTER 360** — Leica scan registration.
- **CloudCompare** — free, open-source point-cloud tools.

## Drafting / document

- **Bluebeam Revu** — paid. PDF markup, takeoffs.

## File converters / utilities

- **LandXML.org** — the open exchange format. Almost every survey tool reads/writes it.
- **GeoTransformer** (NOAA NGS) — free coordinate transformations.
- **OPUS** (NGS) — free post-processed GPS positioning.
