> **TL;DR**
> 1. ★ **Civil 3D Reminders** (civil3dreminders.com) — Tony Cook's blog; the de-facto reference for working Civil 3D users.
> 2. **Being Civil** (beingcivil.com) — long-form posts; corridor and design topics.
> 3. Autodesk's official **Civil Engineering Blog** for release notes and announcements.

## Civil 3D

- ★ **Civil 3D Reminders** — https://civil3dreminders.com
- **Being Civil** — https://beingcivil.com
- **Civil Immersion** — Donnie Gladfelter. Short, practical posts.
- **Cadapult Software blog** — Tutorial posts tied to their training.
- **Engineered Efficiency** — consultancy blog with practical tips.
- **Autodesk "Civil Engineering" blog** — official channel for release notes, announcements.

## Survey & legal

- **The American Surveyor** (xyHt-affiliated) — articles on practice and law.
- **Land Surveyors United** community blog.
- **NSPS news** — https://www.nsps.us.com/news

## Civil engineering general

- **Engineered Efficiency**, **The Civil Engineer Blog** — engineering practice topics.
- **EngineeringNews-Record (ENR)** — industry news.

## RSS-friendly aggregators

- Feedly with the above feeds is the cheapest way to keep up.
