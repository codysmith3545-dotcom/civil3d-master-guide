> **TL;DR**
> 1. ★ **Civil 3D Reminders** (Tony Cook) — short, focused, problem-solving videos. Best single channel for working surveyors and engineers.
> 2. **Being Civil** (Christopher Fugitt) — long-running blog/channel; deep dives into corridor and design topics.
> 3. **Land Surveyors United** — community channel; field methods, equipment, legal-side discussions.

## Civil 3D / engineering

- ★ **Civil 3D Reminders** — Tony Cook. https://www.youtube.com/@Civil3DReminders
- **Being Civil** — Christopher Fugitt. https://www.youtube.com/@BeingCivil
- **CAD Intentions** — Brandon Loehr. AutoCAD-side fundamentals, occasionally Civil 3D.
- **Cadapult Software** — focused on Civil 3D + surveying workflows.
- **The Civil Engineer Channel** — AASHTO-side civil-engineering tutorials.
- **Ron Couillard – Civil 3D** — older but evergreen Civil 3D content.

## Survey-specific

- ★ **Land Surveyors United** — community + interviews + field methods.
- **Surveyor Side Hustle** — small-business surveyor topics.
- **Surveyor Training and Education**, **Survey Legend** — boundary-law and field practice.

## Civil engineering general

- **The Civil Engineering Podcast (video)** — interviews and topic deep-dives.
- **PE Civil Exam Prep** — exam-prep style.

## Equipment & field tech

- **Trimble Geospatial** — manufacturer channel.
- **Carlson Software** — manufacturer channel.
