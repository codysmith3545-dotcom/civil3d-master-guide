> **TL;DR**
> 1. **Mastering AutoCAD Civil 3D** (Sybex) — the standard general-purpose Civil 3D book. **★ start here**.
> 2. **Surveying** (Kavanagh) and **Surveying: Principles and Applications** (Kavanagh) — the most-assigned college survey texts.
> 3. **Brown's Boundary Control and Legal Principles** — the canonical U.S. boundary-law reference.

## Civil 3D

- ★ **Mastering AutoCAD Civil 3D** — Sybex / Wiley. Re-issued for major releases. Covers fundamentals through plan production. Beginner → intermediate.
- **Up and Running with AutoCAD Civil 3D** — Elise Moss. Workbook-style, good for self-study. Beginner.
- **AutoCAD Civil 3D for Surveyors** — Aubrey Goodyear. Survey-specific recipes. Intermediate.

## Surveying (general)

- ★ **Surveying** — Barry Kavanagh, Tom Mastin. Classic textbook. Beginner → intermediate.
- **Surveying: Principles and Applications** — Kavanagh, Mastin, Slattery. Slightly more applied.
- **Elementary Surveying: An Introduction to Geomatics** — Ghilani & Wolf. Stronger on adjustments and theory. Intermediate → advanced.
- **Adjustment Computations: Spatial Data Analysis** — Ghilani. The least-squares reference. Advanced.

## Boundary law

- ★ **Brown's Boundary Control and Legal Principles** — Robillard, Wilson, Brown. The canonical U.S. boundary-law reference. Read cover-to-cover at least once.
- **Evidence and Procedures for Boundary Location** — Robillard, Wilson. Companion volume.
- **Skelton on Boundaries** — older, but cited often in Indiana case law.

## Civil engineering / hydrology

- ★ **Hydrology and Floodplain Analysis** — Bedient, Huber, Vieux. The undergraduate hydrology textbook.
- **Open Channel Hydraulics** — Chow. Classic.
- **Stormwater Management Manual for Western Washington** (Ecology) — free PDF; technically WA-specific but widely used as a reference for BMPs and water quality.

## Roadway design

- ★ **AASHTO Green Book** (A Policy on Geometric Design of Highways and Streets) — the source. Buy from AASHTO; do not pirate.
- **Highway Engineering** — Garber & Hoel. College textbook companion.

## Construction & earthwork

- **Means Heavy Construction Cost Data** — RSMeans. Updated annually. For estimating.
- **Construction Surveying and Layout** — Crawford. Practical, contractor-friendly.
