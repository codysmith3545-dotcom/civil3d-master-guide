> 1. The mathematical and measurement core of any surveying exam: bearings/azimuths, distances, coordinate systems, plane trig, and error theory.
> 2. Master conversion between bearing and azimuth, traverse closure, area by coordinates, and basic least squares concepts before drilling questions.
> 3. The Civil 3D Master Guide has working calculators for most of these topics under `/tools`. Use them to check your hand work.
