> **TL;DR**
> 1. Boundary law is evidence-driven: written record, monuments, occupation, and intent. Most exam questions test which evidence controls in a conflict.
> 2. Indiana adds layers: 865 IAC standards of practice, IC 32-21-7 adverse possession (10 years), IC 36-2-12 county surveyor authority for PLSS corners.
> 3. Senior rights, riparian/littoral rights, and PLSS corner restoration are perennial exam topics.
