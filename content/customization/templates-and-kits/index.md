> **TL;DR**
> 1. A **DWT** is a regular DWG file used as a starting point — it carries all of Civil 3D's settings, styles, layers, page setups. Highest-leverage customization in the product.
> 2. Civil 3D ships with **country kits** (US Imperial, US Metric, AU, UK, etc.) — pick one as your baseline and layer your company overrides. Don't try to build from a blank `acad.dwt`.
> 3. Maintain **one company DWT per discipline** (survey, design, plans). Resist drift — quarterly review.
