> **TL;DR**
> 1. Customization sits on three rails: **LISP** (quick AutoCAD-side automation), **.NET API** (deep Civil 3D automation), **Dynamo** (visual programming for repeatable flows).
> 2. Templates (DWT) are still the highest-leverage customization. Most "we need a tool" is actually "fix the template."
> 3. Country kits (the regional content you pick at install) bundle templates, assemblies, parts lists, label styles for a region (US Imperial, US Metric, etc.). Civilly start there, then layer your company's overrides.
