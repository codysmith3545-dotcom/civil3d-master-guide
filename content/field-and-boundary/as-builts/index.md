> **TL;DR**
> 1. As-builts document **what was actually built**, including deviations from plan. They're often required for occupancy/acceptance and for permit closeout.
> 2. Storm and sanitary as-builts always include **rim, invert, pipe size/material, slope** — and a measured length, not a planned one.
> 3. Tolerances are jurisdiction-dependent. Always ask the reviewing authority what their bar is before you survey.
