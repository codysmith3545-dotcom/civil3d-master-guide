> **TL;DR**
> 1. This section covers the instruments a working surveyor uses daily: total stations, GNSS receivers, levels, data collectors, and scanning systems.
> 2. Each page includes setup procedures, accuracy specifications, and field checks so you can verify your equipment is performing to spec before trusting the data.
> 3. Calibration and maintenance schedules protect your investment and your professional liability.

## How to use this section

These pages are written for surveyors who already understand the basic theory of measurement. The focus is on practical field procedures, manufacturer-specific details where they matter, and the checks that catch problems before they corrupt a dataset.

If you are looking for how Civil 3D processes the data these instruments collect, see the [Civil 3D survey database](../../civil3d/survey/survey-database.md) section.
