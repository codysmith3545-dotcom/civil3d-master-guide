> **TL;DR**
> 1. A topo's purpose is a **TIN that's accurate where it matters**. Most of the work is deciding where breaklines go and where you don't need shots.
> 2. Density rule of thumb: shots dense enough that the TIN matches reality to your contour interval / 2. For 1' contours, shots ≤ 0.5' vertical relief between adjacent points along break direction.
> 3. Always include **utility surface evidence** (rim, invert, surface marks) — if you don't shoot it, the next surveyor will.
