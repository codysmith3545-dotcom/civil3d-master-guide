> **TL;DR**
> 1. **Boundary survey** = retracement of an existing parcel based on deeds, plats, monuments, and senior rights. **ALTA/NSPS** is a specific national standard for title-insurance-grade surveys.
> 2. ALTA "Table A" optional items are negotiated with the client/title company; common picks: 1, 2, 3, 4, 6(b), 7(a), 8, 11, 13, 16, 17, 19.
> 3. The **2021 ALTA/NSPS Land Title Survey Standards** are the current effective version (Feb 23, 2021).
