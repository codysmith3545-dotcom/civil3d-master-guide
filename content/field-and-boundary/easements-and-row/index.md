> **TL;DR**
> 1. **Easement** = right to use; **right-of-way** = a specific kind of easement, usually for transit. Understand the granting instrument before drawing anything.
> 2. **Express easements** are recorded; **implied** and **prescriptive** ones come from facts on the ground. Both matter for surveys.
> 3. ROW dedications happen via plat or separate instrument; check the recorded version, not the plan.
