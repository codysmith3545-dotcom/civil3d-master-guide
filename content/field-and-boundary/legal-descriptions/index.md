> **TL;DR**
> 1. A legal description must **close** mathematically and **describe a unique parcel** unambiguously. Failures of either are deal-breakers.
> 2. Indiana common forms: **metes and bounds**, **section/aliquot** (PLSS), and **lot and block** (recorded plat reference).
> 3. Always include: **commencement → POB → courses → POB closure → acreage → exceptions → easements → reference to source instruments**.
