> **TL;DR**
> 1. Stake offsets, not line. Hubs at offset distance with **cut/fill** marked on the lath; the contractor reads it and pulls back.
> 2. Always carry **redundant control** to the site and check into a known monument before and after staking.
> 3. Save your stake-out files (LandXML or CSV) and a copy of the design version used — you'll be asked.
